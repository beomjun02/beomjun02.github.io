Beomjun Kim

I am a M.S. student at ALIN-LAB, advised by Prof. Jinwoo Shin at KAIST. I received my B.S. in Computer Science from KAIST in 2025.

My research mainly focuses on Robotics Foundation Models, with a broader interest in LLM/VLMs. Recently, I have been interested in human-to-robot transfer — learning dexterous robot policies solely from human demonstrations, without any robot data, to address the data bottleneck in robotics.
